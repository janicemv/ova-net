About Us

OvaNet is a global initiative uniting experts in ovarian tissue cryopreservation to standardize procedures, improve outcomes, and provide fertility preservation solutions worldwide.

  • Standardizing freezing and transplantation protocols
  • Improving the success rates of ovarian tissue preservation
  • Enhancing follow-up care for patients
  • Establishing a global registry for clinical data
  • Collaborating with leading fertility organizations

Our goal for OVA-NET is to make sure that we provide OTC to the patients who benefit the most, and that we make the procedures as effective as possible.

The Need for OVA-NET

For the past 25 years, various centers worldwide have conducted OTC and ovarian tissue transplantation (OTT) with their own protocols. However, there is no standardization in:

  • Freezing protocols
  • Transplantation procedures
  • Cryoprotectants, media, equilibration times, and temperature profiles
  • Surgical approaches for transplantation
  • Reporting of reproductive outcomes

The lack of standardization makes it difficult to compare success rates and evaluate the effectiveness of different techniques. Live birth rates from OTC currently range from 18-41%, highlighting the need for a unified approach.
